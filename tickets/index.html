Ticket Guide:

Tickets are a way for you to get support from staff members!

To create a ticket go into #make-a-ticket and type anything you'd like

Jack or a SrMod+ will reply to your ticket asap!

Once your ticket has been closed, you will get a message from "Jack's Hangout Ticket Feedback"

You will be given a feedback link where you can fill in what you think of the support
