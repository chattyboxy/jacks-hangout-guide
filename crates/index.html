Crates Guide:

Crates are an easy way to make coins daily!

To obtain a crate, go into #crate-claim and type a message for a random amount of crates

The channel itself has a 6 hour slowmode only allowing you to claim once every 6 hours

To open a crate do /opencrate picking the type and amount you want to open

There is a 3.3% (1/30) chance of getting the Crate Collector role
